Blending Art & Technology

Just as DNA forms the fundamental instructions for life, code forms the building blocks of the digital realm. I specialize in building projects that transcend typical static user interfaces, focusing on dynamic interaction.

Whether it's custom mathematical particle canvases, advanced WebGL models, or smooth physics-based animations, my aim is to make visitors feel engaged and immersed. Every design is calculated down to the pixel.

I focus on clean architecture, performance optimization, and custom-tailored designs, ensuring that animations do not compromise speed or loading times.
